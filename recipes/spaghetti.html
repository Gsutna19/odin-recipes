Spaghetti Recipe

This spaghetti recipe is the simplest and most delicious to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 4 oz cream cheese, cubed
  • 8 oz spagghetti, cooked and drained
  • 2 tablespoons grafted parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti as directed on package.
  2. MEANWHILE, brown meat in large skillet. Stir in sauce and cream cheese; cook on low heat for 3 to 5 min, or until sauce is well blended and heated through. Stir frequently.
  3. Drain spaghetti. Add to sauce; mix lightly. Place on platter; top with Parmesan.
