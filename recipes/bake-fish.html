Bake Fish Recipe

This Bake Fish recipe is super easy to make, and it can be served as a main dish or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbspoons of honey
  • 3 tbspoons of Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 4 salmon steaks
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F
  2. In a small bowl, mix honey, mustard, and lemon juice. Spread the mixture over the salmon steaks. Season with pepper. Arrange in a medium baking dish
  3. Bake 20 min in preheated oven, or until fish easily flakes with a fork.
