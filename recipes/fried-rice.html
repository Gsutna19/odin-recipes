Fried Rice Recipe

This fried rice recipe is the perfect option if you want a delicious meal with a perfect texture! It is easy to make since it needs the oven.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 2 tbspoons canola oil
  • 1 tbspoon sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons chile paste(optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Place rice in large baking bowl, drizzle in canola and sesame oils, and toss to coat rice completely. Add garlic, green onions, bell peppers, carrots, peas, and ham. Season with salt. Stir well until combined.
  3. Combine chicken broth, soy sauce, and chile paste in a pot over high heat. Stir to bring to a boil. Pour on top of the rice and stir briefly. Cover top tightly with heavy-duty aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 32 min. Reemove and let stand for 10 min. Uncover, fluff rice with fork. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  5. Increase oven temperature to 475 degrees F. Return to the oven until rice is browned and crusted, about 10 min.
